# Build your first Embeddable

<Pill><span>Takes ~6 mins</span></Pill>

**Create** and **deploy** your first Embeddable dashboard.

<Callout emoji="üí°">
  An **Embeddable** is an interactive dashboard that you embed in your web application.
</Callout>

<Steps>

### **Create a New Embeddable**
On your workspace homepage, click **Create new Embeddable** to create a fresh dashboard.

### **Add a Dataset**

Datasets are how you access data on a dashboard. 

Your workspace is linked to our sample database. 

<VideoComponent
    src="/video/create-new-dataset-example.mp4"
    width="1100"
    height="746"
/>

1. In the **Datasets Panel** in the bottom right, click the **+** icon.
2. Name your dataset.
3. Select **My customers** (or any other sample data model), then **Save**.

### **Add a Donut Chart**

Build your dashboard by adding components to the canvas. 

<VideoComponent
    src="/video/create-pie-chart.mp4"
    width="1100"
    height="746"
/>

1. Click **Add component**. Pick **Donut Chart**.
2. In the edit panel, set **Dataset to display** to the dataset you just created.
3. Select your preferred **Measure** and **Dimension**.
4. Hit **Save**.

<Callout emoji="‚ö°Ô∏è">
**Bonus**: check the **console** in the right sidebar to see what's happening behind the scenes. 
</Callout>

### **Add Interactivity**

Adding interactivity is quick and easy. 

<VideoComponent
    src="/video/Add-interactivity-remarkable.mp4"
    width="1100"
    height="746"
/>

1. Click **Add component**, choose **Date Range Select Field**, then hit **Save**.  

<Callout emoji="‚ö°Ô∏è">
In the right-hand Variables panel in the Builder, you‚Äôll see a new variable appear: **date-range value**. Variables store outputs from interactive components (like date pickers) and can be passed around the dashboard.
</Callout>

2. Edit your **Donut Chart**.  
3. Click **Filter** (under the Dataset input) and, from the dropdown, choose the **Signed up at** Dimension.  
4. Next to the filter operator (‚ÄúIn date range‚Äù), click the **link** icon. 
5. Select the **date-range value** variable, then hit **Save**.

</Steps>

<Callout emoji="ü•≥">
**Success!** Switch to Preview mode in the top bar to test the interaction.
</Callout>

### Embed your dashboard

As a final step, embed your dashboard using the `embedding-preview` script. 

<Steps>

### Save a version

1. Click **'Save version'** at the top of your dashboard.
2. Enter a simple **description** and click **'Save new version'**. 

### Publish the dashboard
3. Click the **'Publish'** button in the top bar. 
4. Select the **'Production'** environment (the green box). 
5. Grab your **API Key** and **Embeddable ID** from the **'How to embed'** section at the bottom of the modal. 
6. Click **'Publish'** to publish your saved version. 

### Test embedding
7. Head to your code repo, open `embedding-preview.cjs` ([here](https://github.com/embeddable-hq/embeddable-boilerplate/blob/main/src/embeddable.com/scripts/embedding-preview.cjs)).  
8. Update the `BASE_URL`, `API_KEY`, and `EMBEDDABLE_ID` with the values from your dashboard. Then run:  

   ```bash
   node src/embeddable.com/scripts/embedding-preview.cjs
   ```
9. Go to [http://localhost:8080/](http://localhost:8080/) to see your embedded dashboard in action! ü§©

</Steps>

Congratulations! You've built your first Embeddable - now you're well on your way to creating amazing, interactive analytics experiences.

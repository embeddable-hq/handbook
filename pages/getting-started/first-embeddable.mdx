# Build your first Embeddable

You're ready to start building! Letâ€™s walk through creating a **basic dashboard** in Embeddable - from adding your first component to embedding the result.

<Callout emoji="ðŸ’¡">
  An **Embeddable** is an interactive dashboard that you embed in your web application.
</Callout>

<Steps>

### **Create a New Embeddable**
- On your workspace homepage, click **Create new Embeddable** to create a fresh dashboard.

### **Add a Text Component**
1. Click **Add component** (top right).
2. Scroll down and choose **Text component**.
3. Fill in **Title** and **Body**, then hit **Save**.

<VideoComponent
    src="/video/add-text-component.mp4"
    width="1250"
    height="872"
/>

<Callout emoji="ðŸ’¡">
  The available components come from your code repository.
</Callout>

### **Add a Dataset**
1. In the **Datasets** panel (bottom right), click the **+** icon.
2. Name your dataset, select **My customers** (or any other sample data model), then **Save**.

<VideoComponent
    src="/video/add-dataset.mp4"
    width="1250"
    height="872"
/>

### **Add a Pie Chart**
1. Click **Add component** again. Pick **Pie Chart**.
2. In the edit panel, set **Dataset to display** to the dataset you just created.
3. Select your preferred **Slice** and **Metric**.
4. **Save** and position your pie chart on the canvas.

<VideoComponent
    src="/video/add-pie-chart.mp4"
    width="1250"
    height="872"
/>

### **Add Interactivity**

Let's add a **search input** that filters the data. Watch and follow this quick video:

<ExternalVideo src="https://www.loom.com/embed/041f9947644245d5bed940a2d46bc6ca?sid=b3d41e99-9f69-440c-8e93-4174e95c0f60"/>

### **Embed Your Dashboard**
1. Click **Publish** at the top of your dashboard.
2. In the pop-up, copy your **API Key** and **Embeddable ID**.

<ImageGrid images={["/img/api-key.png"]} />

3. In your code repo, open `embedding-preview.cjs` (inside `scripts`).  
4. Update the `BASE_URL`, `API_KEY`, and `EMBEDDABLE_ID` with the values from your dashboard.  
5. Run:
   ```bash
   node src/embeddable.com/scripts/embedding-preview.cjs
   ```
6. Go to [http://localhost:8080/](http://localhost:8080/) to see your embedded dashboard in action!

</Steps>

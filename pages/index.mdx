## Why Embeddable?

Embeddable helps you build remarkable analytics experiences inside your own product - **no clunky iframe** in sight. It marries **no-code dashboards** with the **full power** of your codebase, empowering your team to **build**, **edit**, **deploy**, and **scale** data-experiences for your customers.

1. **Native Feel**  
   Drop a simple web component (`<em-beddable>`) into your site or app, and let Embeddable handle the rest. No iframes or unstyled add-ons - your dashboards adopt the look and feel of your product.

2. **No-Code + Full-Code**  
   Non-technical users drag and drop charts, filters, and controls, while developers retain control over React components, data models, and row-level security.

3. **Secure & Row-Level Aware**  
   Generate security tokens to **strictly** control which data each user sees. Filter out sensitive rows, apply schema-level security, or switch entire databases per user if needed.

4. **Model Data Once**  
   Define your metrics, dimensions, and transformations in code. The no-code builder then exposes these for easy selection - no repeated SQL queries or one-off scripts.

5. **Performance & Scalability**  
   Leverage caching, pre-aggregations, and advanced queries automatically. Keep your data fast, even at scale.

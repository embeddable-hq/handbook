# Self-serve analytics

Give your users more than static dashboards. **Self-serve** lets your end-users **explore data and answer their own questions** directly inside your product — without waiting on your engineering or analyst teams. You stay in control of the data, metrics, and guardrails; your users get the freedom to dig in.

## Why self-serve?

- **Personalized insights, faster** → Users answer their own questions without waiting on a dev cycle.
- **Freedom within guardrails** → You define the datasets, metrics, and components; they explore with confidence.
- **Native and secure** → [Row-level security](/data-modeling/row-level-security/introduction) ensures each user only sees their own data, and everything is themed to feel like part of your app.
